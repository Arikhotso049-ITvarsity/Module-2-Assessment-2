JUMANJI

Enter the game and survive the wild challenges.

Genre: Adventure / Fantasy

Release Year: 2017

Rating: PG-13

Synopsis:
"Jumanji" is a thrilling adventure where four teenagers are transported into a dangerous jungle video game. To escape, they must complete the game's challenges while facing wild animals, treacherous landscapes, and their own fears.

The movie features exciting action, hilarious moments, and powerful teamwork as the players learn to trust each other and find their way back to the real world.
